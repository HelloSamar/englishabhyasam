The Journey

How it started. Where it's going.

This page is Samar's personal log — why he built this, what's been done, and what's coming next. It's a window into the process, not just the product.

The Why

I was looking for what you're looking for.

I am Samar. I am preparing for a government exam. Like most aspirants, I spent a lot of time looking for a structured, free English resource — something that had grammar theory, vocabulary lists, and mock tests all in one place, well-organised, and easy to navigate.

I didn't find it. So I decided to build it. Not after my exam. Right now, alongside my preparation.

English Abhyasam is that resource. It exists because I believe no aspirant should have to pay for quality English preparation. Every rupee should go into preparation, not coaching fees.
