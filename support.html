Hi, I'm Samar.

I'm a government exam aspirant — preparing for my own exam while building this platform at the same time. I started English Abhyasam because I couldn't find a single free, well-organised English resource built for students like me. So I built one.

About this platform

Not a product. A mission.

English Abhyasam is free. It will stay free. No ads, no paywalls — at least not yet. Every topic, every mock test, every vocab list is built by one person: me, between my own study sessions, late nights, and weekends.

The goal is simple — every aspirant, regardless of background or money, should have access to quality English preparation.
